History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local meetup and has grown into a globally recognized tech event. From AI innovations to web development breakthroughs, TechCon has hosted countless cutting-edge showcases.

Mission

TechCon’s mission is to unite thinkers, doers, and visionaries to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and shape the future of technology.
